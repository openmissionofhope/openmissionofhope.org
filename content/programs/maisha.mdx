Maisha is an open library for practical mercy. People in Sub-Saharan Africa lead the curation; we host the infrastructure and uphold transparent standards.

## Our Approach

Knowledge is most effective when it's contextual. Maisha empowers regional editors to curate health, water, sanitation, and hygiene resources that work in their communities—grounded in both evidence and faith.

## What We Cover

- Community health best practices
- Water and sanitation solutions
- Nutrition and food security
- Disease prevention and response
- Traditional wisdom integrated with modern approaches

## Leadership

Regional editors from Sub-Saharan Africa lead content curation, ensuring cultural relevance and practical applicability.
