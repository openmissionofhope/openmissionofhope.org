We support local coordinators to reduce overdose deaths with training, awareness, and distribution transparency.

## Our Mission

The opioid crisis demands a compassionate, community-based response. NaloxoneForAll equips local leaders to save lives through education, access to naloxone kits, and transparent reporting of our impact.

## What We Provide

- **Training**: Evidence-based overdose recognition and response training
- **Kits**: Coordinated naloxone kit distribution through community partners
- **Transparency**: Public reporting of distribution and outcomes
- **Support**: Resources for community coordinators and volunteers

## Who We Serve

We partner with churches, community centers, harm reduction organizations, and local leaders in the US and Canada who are committed to reducing overdose deaths with dignity and compassion.

## Get Involved

Whether you want to become a community coordinator, volunteer, or support this work financially, there's a place for you in this life-saving mission.
